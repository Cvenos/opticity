Opticity is a simple repo that provides Cydia/Sileo tweaks on it. I try to have only iOS 12 Jailbraek compatible tweaks by pwn20wnd. You can also always contact me if you think a tweak should be removed or have any suggestions for tweaks to add. If there are any developers out there seeing this, you may make a request to have your tweak on my repo for completely free. I am not affiliated with any of the creators of any of these tweaks.
